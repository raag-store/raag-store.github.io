About Raag

Our Passion for Handloom

At Raag, we believe in the enduring beauty and artistry of hand-woven textiles. Our mission is to bring you a curated selection of authentic sarees, crafted with skill and care by traditional weavers.


We celebrate the rich heritage of Indian handlooms, offering sarees in natural fabrics like Cotton, Silk, and Linen, as well as elegant Blends and contemporary designs. Each piece tells a story of tradition and craftsmanship.


Temporarily Closed: We are currently taking a temporary break. We deeply appreciate the support and enthusiasm our customers have shown for Raag and the art of handloom. We are working towards reopening and will announce updates soon.


Thank you for your understanding and continued interest in Raag. We look forward to sharing our passion for hand-woven sarees with you again in the future.
